Chili con carne

Description

Although it apparently does not photograph well, chilli con carne is one of my favourite meals.

It's super easy to make if you want, or you can spend hours and hours on really deepening the flavour.

Ingredients

  • Beef mince
  • Kidney beans
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Green pepper
  • Garlic
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Oregano
  • Cumin
  • Chocolate powder
  • Lime
  • Coriander (to garnish)

Steps

  1. Brown the mince in a pan over medium heat. Don't touch it much to get a good crust on the meat. Once cooked, set aside for later.
  2. Cook the onion, celery and pepper until softened, then add garlic.
  3. Deglaze the pan with beer. When it has mostly evaporated, add tomatoes, kidney beans and stock.
  4. Add cayenne, cumin, oregano and chocolate. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Ensure that everything is covered (add more water if not). Bring to the boil and let simmer for as long as you like - ideally 45 minutes, but the longer the better. Continue to top up with water as necessary.
  6. When ready to serve, add juice of a lime and salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve over rice.
