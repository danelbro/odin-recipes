Chili con carne

Description

Although it apparently does not photograph well, chilli con carne is one of my favourite meals.

It's super easy to make if you want, or you can spend hours and hours on really deepening the flavour.

Ingredients

  • Beef mince
  • Kidney beans
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Green pepper
  • Garlic
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Oregano
  • Cumin
  • Chocolate powder
  • Lime
  • Coriander (to garnish)
