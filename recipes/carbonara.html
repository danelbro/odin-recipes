Carbonara

Description

This classic Roman pasta dish is notoriously difficult to master.

It's a deliciously creamy sauce, with crisp fatty pancetta and flavoured with lots of freshly ground black epper, but uses no cream; the creaminess comes from the eggs.

Here's a method I've honed for a few years, which seems pretty authentic.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti or linguine
  • Large eggs - for 2 servings, 3 yolks + 1 whole egg
  • Pancetta
  • Parmesan
  • Pecorino
  • Pepper
  • Butter
