Mushroom risotto

Description

Another Italian classic. Creamy rice with earthy mushrooms.

People say you need to stir this the whole time, but I've heard conflicting things.

Ingredients

  • Risotto rice (usually arborio)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Mushrooms, sliced
  • Chicken stock (or vegetable stock for a vegetarian version)
  • White wine
  • Parmesan
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Cook the mushrooms until golden. Reserve until later.
  2. Cook the onions until softened in the butter. When they are close to done, add the garlic and return the mushrooms to the pan.
  3. Add the rice to the pan and allow it to be coated in the fat.
  4. Deglaze the pan with the wine.
  5. When most of the wine has cooked off, start adding stock bit by bit while stirring. Add more stock when most of it has been absorbed by the rice.
  6. When the rice is cooked, add some more butter and a good amount of grated parmesan.
  7. Serve with shavings of parmesan and a drizzle of extra-virgin olive oil.
